# **App Name**: Furnishr

## Core Features:

- Homepage Hero Banner: Rotating banner showcasing new products, promotions, and key selling points.
- Product Listing and Filtering: Browse and filter products by category, price, style, and material.
- Product Detail Pages: Detailed information on product pages, including high-quality images, descriptions, dimensions, and customer reviews.
- AI-Powered Style Matcher: Use the customer's uploaded photos of their living space as a tool to offer furniture recommendations powered by AI.
- Shopping Cart: An interface that allows you to temporarily store a list of articles you intend to purchase.
- About Us Page: Detailed overview of the store's mission, values, and history.
- Blog Section: Regularly updated blog with interior design tips, furniture care advice, and styling guides.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Muted terracotta (#C97A57) for warmth and sophistication.
- Background color: Soft beige (#F5F5DC), almost white.
- Accent color: Mustard yellow (#D4A773) to highlight key interactive elements.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, for elegance. Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for readability.
- Clean, minimalist icons for navigation and product features.
- Grid-based layout for a clean and organized presentation of products.
- Subtle fade-in animations for product images and section transitions.
